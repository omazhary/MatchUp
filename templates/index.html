MatchUp

Welcome to MatchUp! MatchUp lets you and your buddies who like to engage in 1 vs. 1 activities to generate competing player pairs. The pairs are randomly generated, with the guarantee that each round each participant will get to play against another participant. To get started, type in the names of the participants, and click the "Generate Pairs" button. You will receive of a list of pairs for the entire round, along with recommended referees should your activity require arbitration. Enjoy!
